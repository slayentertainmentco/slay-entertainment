Before You Enquire
Common Questions
How far in advance should I book?
For Sydney events, most clients book 4–6 weeks ahead. Sunshine Coast events require a minimum of 6 weeks notice. For Bali bookings, we ask for at least 3 months lead time to coordinate travel and logistics. Peak dates — New Year's Eve, major race weekends, school holiday periods — book out early, so the sooner you enquire the better.
What's included in each package?
Every booking includes custom costuming, a pre-event brief and run sheet, direct coordination with Abira, and choreographed performance depending on client needs. All performers are ABN registered and Slay holds public liability insurance.
Do you perform outside Sydney?
Yes. Slay Entertainment performs across Sydney, Brisbane, the Sunshine Coast, and Bali. Interstate and international bookings are also considered for the right event. Travel requirements are factored into your custom quote.
Can you tailor a package to our theme or brand?
Absolutely. Custom theming, brand-aligned costuming, and bespoke choreography are core to what we do. Tell us your concept in the enquiry form and we'll come back with options tailored specifically to your event.
How do I get a quote?
Fill in the form below with your event details — date, type, location, and anything you have in mind. We respond to every enquiry within 24 hours with a tailored proposal. No commitment required.
Are you available for regular weekly venue bookings?
Yes. We work with nightclubs and hospitality venues on weekly or fortnightly booking arrangements. Regular venue clients receive priority scheduling and consistent performer pairing. Enquire with your preferred dates and we'll put together a proposal.
